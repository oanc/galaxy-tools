Session Time Out

If you are signed into your Inbox, but are not actively using it, the session may expire. The default time out setting is 30 minutes, but may vary in your environment. If you are timed out, the Sign In page appears when you select Refresh.

Note: Be sure to save your task output data after you enter it, so that the changes are not lost if your session times out. For details, see Working with a Work Item, Attachments and Comments.
